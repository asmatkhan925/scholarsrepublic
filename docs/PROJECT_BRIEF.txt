Scholars Republic is a Pakistan-first scholarship matching and study-abroad guidance platform that is being built as a flexible student opportunity platform.

Scholarships are the first MVP opportunity type. Future opportunity types can include jobs, internships, fellowships, exchange programs, admissions, competitions, training, mentorship programs, and research positions.

MVP focus:
- Authentication
- Student profile
- Opportunity database foundation
- Search and filters
- Rule-based match score
- Saved opportunities
- Application tracker
- Document checklist
- Service requests
- Blog/guides

Do not build jobs UI, internships UI, AI chatbot, mobile app, automated payments, consultant dashboard, university dashboard, community features, or ML recommendations in the MVP.

Primary positioning:
"A Pakistan-first scholarship and study-abroad matching platform where students create a profile, discover verified scholarships, check eligibility, track deadlines, prepare documents, and apply with confidence."

This repository is developed phase by phase. Phase 4 completed the opportunity database foundation with scholarships stored as opportunity records.
